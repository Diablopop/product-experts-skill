## How to Use This Example

### 1. Read the Summary First

Start with [summary.md](summary.md) to get:
- **Executive Summary** - High-level synthesis (2-3 paragraphs)
- **Common Themes** - What most experts agreed on
- **Divergent Perspectives** - Where experts had different views
- **Key Takeaways & Action Items** - 5-7 concrete recommendations
- **Next Steps** - Suggested actions

This gives you the big picture without reading all 5 expert responses.

### 2. Dive Into Specific Experts

Based on the summary, read individual expert responses that resonate:

- **[Wes Kao](experts/wes-kao.md)** - Managing up and communication strategies
- **[Andy Raskin](experts/andy-raskin.md)** - Strategic narrative and positioning frameworks
- **[Christina Wodtke](experts/christina-wodtke.md)** - OKRs, strategy execution, and team rituals
- **[Shreyas Doshi](experts/shreyas-doshi.md)** - Product strategy and high-agency PM leadership
- **[Nickey Skarstad](experts/nickey-skarstad.md)** - Strategy development and stakeholder alignment

Each expert file includes:
- Background and bio
- Key insights specific to the question
- Actionable advice
- Direct quotes from their podcast interview
- Related topics they covered

### 3. Apply the Advice

Use the "Key Takeaways & Action Items" section to:
- Identify 1-2 recommendations to implement immediately
- Create your own action plan
- Reference specific expert advice when needed

## Questions This Example Might Raise

**Q: Why these 5 experts for this question?**
A: The skill scored all 302 experts based on keyword overlap with the question ("strategic stability", "leadership", "changing direction", "roadmap shifts", "morale"). These 5 had the highest relevance scores considering expertise areas and interview topics.

**Q: Can I get different experts?**
A: Yes, ask your question differently or more specifically. For example, "How do I influence my CEO to commit to quarterly goals?" would likely select different experts with influencing and stakeholder management experience.

**Q: Is all output this detailed?**
A: Yes, the skill always consults 5 experts and creates similar depth. The specificity depends on how relevant each expert's interview was to your question.

**Q: How long does this take?**
A: ~2-3 minutes total. The 5 expert analyses run in parallel, so it's much faster than sequential.
